Welcome to SchoolPay Gateway

SchoolPay Gateway is a premier payment solution crafted for international schools, delivering a secure, efficient, and scalable platform to manage financial transactions across diverse currencies and regions. Our Key Features

Secure Payment Processing

Leveraging advanced encryption and compliance with PCI DSS standards, we safeguard every transaction against fraud and data breaches with real-time monitoring.

Multi-Currency Support
